Projects

Here are a few projects I have worked on, demonstrating a real world application of my skills!

unify.fm

This app solves the problem and nuisance of having to visit each individual internet radio station's websites to listen to them and see who's playing. Now you can easily navigate through internet radio stations with an easy to use music player that mimics the functionality of a car radio. You can favorite stations, scan through your favorited stations. Browsing what station to listen to becomes a lot easier by displaying all of the "Now Playing" information in one place, now you won't have to miss your favorite radio shows!

React Redux Flask SQLAlchemy

  • Visit unify.fm

disco-ord

Disco-ord is a disco themed clone of Discord. You can create channels & servers, add/remove members to servers, and chat with other members.This app features instant messaging, utilizing web sockets.

React Redux Flask SQLAlchemy socketIO

  • Visit disco-ord

soundDrone

This app is a music posting platform inspired by SoundCloud, where you can upload songs, add them to playlists, and listen to songs/playlists with a built in queue feature. The aesthetics are inspired by drone music.
